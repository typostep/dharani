MOVIES


A movie is a visual art that is used to communicate ideas, stories, perceptions, feelings, etc. through the use of
moving images. These images are accompanied by sound and other effects. Over the generations we have seen different
movies in different genres. Movies have evolved a lot since their first appearance. A lot of changes took place in
the appearance as well as the content of movies.

MOST POPULAR MOVIE GENRES ALL OVER THE WORLD

  • Action
  • Drama
  • Comedy
  • Adventure
  • Thriller
  • Horror
  • Romcom
Movies have been a great source of employment and entertainment ever since they have been invented. However there are
disadvantages too!!! It always depends on how we humans accept any new inventions. Movies have always been entertaining.
They have been providing employment to number of people all over the world. They have brought fame to lots and lots of
people. They have motivated masses in many ways through many films. They have taught a lot of new things to people. They
led to the growth of channels too!! Sometimes movies can promote false notions as well. They can mislead people especially
the youth. Hence movies have both pros and cons. It always depends on us to think what has to be accepted and what not to be!!
